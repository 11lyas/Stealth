FAQ

Common questions.

How accurate is the AI's analysis?
Within ±20% of actual market data when verified. We surface confidence intervals on every projection (e.g., "$32k–$44k Y1 cash flow") and cite every source — RentCast, AirDNA, public records. No black-box single-point estimates.
Is this financial or legal advice?
No. Vesta is a research tool that organizes public data. Every report ends with a "verify before offer" checklist (inspection, ordinance check, HOA review). Always consult a real estate attorney, CPA, and inspector before purchasing.
What data sources do you use?
Active listings + sold comps from RentCast and Realtor.com. Short-term rental performance from AirDNA + verified Airbnb calendars. Regulation data from city ordinance pages and HOA records. Every data point is footnoted with its source.
Does it work for long-term rentals or only Airbnb?
Short-term rentals (Airbnb, VRBO) at launch. Long-term rental analysis is on the roadmap and ships within 90 days of launch. The same engine — different data sources and projection logic.
What if my market isn't covered?
We cover all 50 US states. Some smaller markets have fewer comps — when that happens, we lower the confidence score and flag it instead of guessing. If you can't get a confident analysis, you don't pay.
How is this different from AirDNA or Mashvisor?
Those tools give you raw data dashboards. Vesta uses AI to reason about that data — synthesizing comps, regulation, seasonality, and 10-year cash flow into a single answer with citations. We don't replace AirDNA, we make sense of it.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account in two clicks — no calls, no support tickets. 14-day free trial, then 30-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month.
How is my data handled?
Your searches and saved properties are private — only you see them. We never sell user data. We use Stripe (payments), Supabase (auth + database), and Anthropic Claude (AI) — all SOC 2 compliant. Full privacy policy on the Privacy page.

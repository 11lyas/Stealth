FAQ

Common questions.

How accurate is the AI's analysis?
Vesta is for landlords who already own a long-term rental and want to know if converting to an Airbnb is worth it. We're not for first-time buyers hunting properties to acquire, and we're not a "where should I buy" tool. We serve the LTR-to-STR transition, end-to-end.
How is this different from AirDNA or Hostaway?
AirDNA gives you market data assuming you're already an STR investor. Hostaway runs your STR after you have one. Neither bridges the LTR landlord becoming an STR operator — that's our entire focus. We use AirDNA's data tier and Hostaway's category as a partner, not a competitor.
How accurate are the projections?
Within ±20% of actual market data when verified. We surface confidence ranges on every number (e.g., "$38k–$58k STR projection") and cite every source — RentCast, AirDNA, verified calendars. Once you convert, we track real performance vs our projection — so our model gets more accurate every month.
Is this financial or legal advice?
No. Vesta is a research + ops tool that organizes public data and gives you a setup playbook. Always confirm regulations with your city, talk to a CPA about tax implications, and check HOA bylaws before converting. We surface the math, you make the call.
What if my city doesn't allow STRs?
We tell you upfront — that's literally the first thing we check. Top 50 STR markets are hand-curated by a paralegal with quarterly verification. Long-tail cities use AI parsing of public ordinances with strict source citation. If your city has banned or heavily restricted STRs, we say so before you spend a minute on the rest.
What does the setup playbook actually include?
Furnishing list with budget breakdown, photographer recommendations in your city, AI-drafted listing title + description, dynamic pricing tool guidance, STR-friendly insurance brokers, compliance walkthrough (license, permits, taxes), first-30-days operating playbook, common pitfalls. Built so a first-time STR operator doesn't need a $2k course.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account in two clicks — no calls, no support tickets. 14-day free trial, then 30-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month.
How is my data handled?
Your searches and saved properties are private — only you see them. We never sell user data. We use Stripe (payments), Supabase (auth + database), and Anthropic Claude (AI) — all SOC 2 compliant. Full privacy policy on the Privacy page.
